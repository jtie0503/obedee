General Information

Dr. Abdulrahman Al Marzouki is a Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon at Obeid Specialized Hospital in Riyadh, holding the FRCSEd (Fellowship of the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh) qualification. He practices in the Orthopedic Department alongside other noted consultants.

Education & Certification

He has earned Fellowship (FRCSEd) from the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh, a credential recognized internationally for surgical excellence. This indicates completion of formal surgical training and rigorous examination standards.

Professional Experience

Serves in the Orthopedic Department at Obeid Specialized Hospital in Riyadh, providing treatment in areas such as musculoskeletal surgery and trauma care. He is listed among other orthopedic professionals at the hospital.
